Good questions

The things families ask first.

Does my parent need a smartphone or the internet? +

No. Hey Zawo calls the regular telephone they already use, landline or cell. Nothing to download, charge, or learn. If they can answer the phone, they can use it.

Would Mom or Dad really talk to an AI? +

Almost everyone warms up within the first few seconds. The call feels like a natural, unhurried chat with someone genuinely interested, and Hey Zawo always says who it is. No pressure, no rush.

How does the scam protection work? +

Because Hey Zawo talks with your parent every day, it is the first to notice when a new friend starts asking for money or a story does not add up, and it tells you that day. It also helps keep a family safe word fresh, so your parent can verify a caller even when a voice sounds exactly like a loved one. It is early warning and awareness, not account freezing.

What language does it speak? +

English or Spanish. You choose per parent, warm and patient in both.

Who can see the stories and reports? +

Only the family members you invite. Everything is private by default and belongs to your family, to keep, download, or turn into a book whenever you like.
